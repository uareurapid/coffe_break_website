What it does

Everything your caffeine
needs to thrive

A precision tool for coffee lovers who want to feel great all day — and actually sleep at night.

Live caffeine curve

Watch your caffeine level tick down in real time. A dynamic graph shows exactly where you are on your metabolism curve — focus zone, plateau, and sleep-safe window.

Smart break scheduling

Set your active days, preferred break times, and coffee habit. The app auto-schedules your next cup and sends a notification when it's the perfect time to brew.

Guided break timer

When it's time, a focused break screen counts down your brew. Ambient sounds mark the start and end, keeping you present and refreshed before heading back.

Coffee type profiles

Espresso, brewed, cold brew, or decaf — each type has a scientifically accurate caffeine profile. The app calculates your curve based on what you actually drink.

Jitter & sleep alerts

The app warns you when you're approaching jitter territory and shows your projected sleep clearance time — so you always know if that late afternoon cup is worth it.

Fully configurable schedule

Choose your active weekdays, coffees per day, break duration, and how far ahead you want your reminder. Your coffee habit, your rules.

The science

Your caffeine isn't
a mystery anymore

Using the standard pharmacokinetic model, the app simulates caffeine decay in your bloodstream — updated live, every second.

Focus zone (>220 mg)
Where your alertness and cognition peak. The app helps you schedule breaks so you stay here when you need it most.
Winding down (20–220 mg)
Caffeine is still present but declining. A good time to consider your next cup or let it fade naturally toward sleep.
Sleep-safe (<20 mg)
Caffeine has metabolized enough that sleep quality won't be disrupted. The app shows you exactly when you'll reach this point.
